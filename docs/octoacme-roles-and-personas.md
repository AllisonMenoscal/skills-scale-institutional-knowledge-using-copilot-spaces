# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads own quality strategy and standards across the project. They coordinate test execution, validate acceptance criteria, and ensure that delivered features meet defined quality gates before release.

### Responsibilities
- Define quality standards and test strategies aligned with project scope
- Coordinate test planning and execution across the team
- Validate that features meet acceptance criteria before sign-off
- Report defects and track quality metrics throughout the project lifecycle
- Collaborate with Developers to identify testability issues early
- Mentor team members on quality best practices

### Goals
- Ensure consistent, measurable quality standards
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Enable faster feedback cycles and reduce rework
- Build a quality-first culture across the delivery team

### Typical Communication
- Quality reviews in sprint planning and execution meetings
- Test reports and defect summaries for stakeholders
- Collaboration with Developers on testability and test design
- Sign-off gates and release readiness checklists

### Key Interactions
- **With Developers:** Partner to ensure testability during design and implementation; provide feedback on test coverage and quality
- **With Project Manager:** Coordinate QA activities in the timeline and escalate quality blockers
- **With Product Manager:** Support acceptance criteria validation and feature sign-off decisions

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers create intuitive, user-centered interfaces and experiences. They validate design decisions through user research and ensure that implemented features meet usability standards.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and gather requirements from stakeholders
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Validate design solutions with users and stakeholders
- Collaborate with Developers to ensure design feasibility and implementation quality
- Establish and enforce usability standards across the product
- Document design decisions and rationale for future reference

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered, intuitive experiences that drive adoption
- Reduce friction in key user workflows
- Maintain design consistency and brand standards
- Improve accessibility and inclusive design practices

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions with stakeholders
- Design specifications and component documentation for Developers
- User research findings and usability test results
- Accessibility and design system guidelines

### Key Interactions
- **With Product Manager:** Collaborate on requirements, feature prioritization, and user validation
- **With Developers:** Support implementation, conduct design reviews, and iterate on feasibility
- **With Project Manager:** Coordinate design timelines and dependencies in the delivery schedule

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate all release planning, documentation, and deployment activities. They ensure compliance with checklists, manage rollback plans, and communicate release status to all stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate release planning and timeline alignment across teams
- Develop and maintain release checklists and deployment procedures
- Ensure all acceptance criteria are met before deploying to production
- Document release notes, migration steps, and known issues
- Manage rollback plans and incident response procedures
- Communicate release status and deployment windows to stakeholders
- Verify post-deployment functionality and performance

### Goals
- Ensure predictable, low-risk releases to production
- Minimize deployment-related incidents and downtime
- Maintain clear communication with all stakeholders
- Reduce time-to-recovery in case of deployment issues

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness reports and deployment timelines
- Release notes and stakeholder announcements
- Post-deployment verification reports
- Incident escalations and rollback decisions

### Key Interactions
- **With Project Manager:** Set release timelines and coordinate dependencies; escalate release blockers
- **With QA Lead:** Obtain sign-off on quality gates and test coverage before production deployment
- **With Developers:** Validate deployment procedures and coordinate rollback if needed
- **With Stakeholders:** Communicate release windows, status updates, and business impact

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business needs and technical delivery. They gather and document requirements, model workflows, and validate that implemented features deliver the intended business value.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct interviews and workshops to elicit business requirements
- Document requirements and create process/workflow models
- Create acceptance criteria aligned with business objectives
- Validate implemented features against business requirements
- Identify gaps or misalignments between design and business intent
- Support UAT planning and execution
- Document decisions and business rules for future reference

### Goals
- Ensure solutions solve the intended business problem
- Reduce rework due to misaligned requirements
- Enable clearer communication between business and technical teams
- Support post-delivery measurement of business impact

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and process maps
- Acceptance criteria refinement sessions
- Business validation and UAT coordination
- Post-delivery impact analysis and lessons learned

### Key Interactions
- **With Product Manager:** Partner to refine requirements and prioritize backlog items
- **With Developers:** Provide detailed specifications and support implementation questions
- **With QA Lead:** Support acceptance criteria validation and test strategy alignment
